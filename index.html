About Me

Matthew Knetsch was born in Denver in 1995. He spent the first 18 years of his life living in the small mountain town of Evergreen. He attended Montessori schools from preschool through middle school, eventually transitioning to public school when he attended Evergeen Senior High School. test


After graduating high school in 2013, Matthew attended the University of Arizona in Tucson. he graduated with a Bachelors degree in History with a minor in Classics in May of 2017.
